About

This website serves as a digital portfolio highlighting environmental issues within a South African context. It is an assortment of content created for previous assignments relating to this theme and new content created specifically for this portfolio. The things encompassed include: anthropocentric design, pollution, genetically modified food, the consumption of microplastics, and fishing practices in South Africa.
